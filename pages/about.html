Welcome to ESL-ent!
ESL-ent was created to be able to unite existing ESL programs into a more dynamic learning experience.
Here at ESL-ent, we know that not everybody learns at the same pace, or by using the same techniques.
Being able to explore several different options can accelerate learning for our users.
With your ESL-ent buddy at your side, learning English is easier than ever!
